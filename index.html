About me

My full name is Kornelis Sietsma - I tend to use "Kornelis" for formal correspondance, and "Korny" the rest of the time.

I hail from Melbourne, Australia, but now I live and work in the UK.

I work as a principal engineer, troublemaker, and general wise-guy for ThoughtWorks, a global software company.
